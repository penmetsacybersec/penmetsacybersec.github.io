About Trainer

VENKAT PENMETSA is a Cyber Security Trainer helping students understand real-world cyber threats and build skills to protect systems and data. His sessions focus on practical demonstrations and real-time attack scenarios.

Workshop Objectives

  • Understand real-world cyber attacks
  • Learn how to protect personal and organizational data
  • Gain awareness about cyber crimes
  • Explore career opportunities in cybersecurity

Syllabus

  • Networking Basics (OSI Model, IP Address, Ports)
  • Footprinting & Nmap Scanning
  • SQL Injection Basics
  • Phishing & Social Engineering
  • WiFi Security Risks
  • Password Security Techniques

Live Demonstrations

Students will experience real-time cybersecurity demonstrations including scanning networks, phishing simulations, and password cracking in a controlled lab environment.
